About Us

X75 AI is a specialized company in artificial intelligence solutions based in Alain, dedicated to supporting local and international companies in adopting cutting-edge AI technologies in a structured and practical way.

We focus on enabling digital transformation, building AI-powered solutions, establishing partnerships with relevant technology partners, structuring opportunities and projects, and supporting execution until transformative results are achieved.

What differentiates us from others is that we don't provide theoretical solutions alone. Instead, we focus on bridging strategy with implementation, backed by a deep understanding of market dynamics, technological landscape, and the way to engage with government, semi-government, and private sector entities.
